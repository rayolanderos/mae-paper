How Users Interact With Mae

MÃ¤e is simple to interact with using an Amazon Echo device or via our website. Users can record the following types of journals using the Alexa voice application:

# Journal Type Voice Command Description
1 Text Post a journal Post a new text journal entry. Mae will analyze the past two weeks worth of entries and provide customized advice as needed.
2 Text (no advice) Add a journal with no advice Post a new text journal entry, but do not receive advice based on the input.
3 Advice Give me some random advice Mae will give you random advice from its advice store.
4 Height measurement Record a new height Record a new height in inches for your baby.
5 Weight measurement Record a new weight Record a new weight in pounds for your baby.
6 Sleep time Record a new sleep time Record a new length of time for your baby's sleep in hours.
7 Diaper Record a new diaper Record a new diaper and its contents for your baby.
